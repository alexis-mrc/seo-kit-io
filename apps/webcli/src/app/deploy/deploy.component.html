Why firebase?

Using Firebase to host a static, simple website offers several advantages:

  • Firebase Hosting provides robust capabilities for hosting static websites, ensuring fast and reliable performance globally with its content delivery network (CDN).
  • It simplifies the deployment process through SEO Kit, allowing easy deployment of your website using CLI commands or through automated workflows.
  • Firebase Hosting ensures security with default SSL encryption for all traffic, offering peace of mind for handling sensitive data.
  • Moreover, Firebase Hosting supports seamless integration with custom domains, enabling you to use your branded domain name effortlessly.

In summary, Firebase Hosting is an excellent choice for hosting static websites due to its speed, reliability, security features, and support for custom domain integration, making it ideal for both personal and business use cases.

No firebase account? Create one
  1. Go to the Firebase Website
    • Open your browser and go to the Firebase website.
  2. Click on "Get Started"
    • On the Firebase homepage, click the "Get started" button to begin the sign-up process.
  3. Sign in with a Google Account
    • If you already have a Google account, enter your email and password to sign in.
    • If you do not have a Google account, click on "Create account" and follow the prompts to create one.
  4. Accept the Terms of Service
    • Read and accept the Firebase and Google terms of service.
  5. Create a firebase project
    • Once signed in, you will have to create Firebase project.
No project? Create a firebase project
  1. Go to the Firebase Console
    • Open your browser and go to the Firebase Console.
  2. Log in to Your Google Account
    • Use your Google credentials to log in if you are not already logged in.
  3. Click on "Add Project"
    • On the Firebase Console homepage, click the "Add project" button.
  4. Enter Project Details
    • Enter a name for your project and click "Continue".
    • Optionally, you can edit the project ID or accept the default.
  5. Refuse Google Analytics (Optional)
    • To optimize performance, Google Analytics is not handled by Seo Kit. If you want it, you can still install it later in your project.
  6. Accept the Terms
    • Review and accept the Firebase terms and conditions.
    • Click "Create project" to proceed.
  7. Wait for the Project to Be Created
    • Firebase will take a few moments to create your project. Once completed, click "Continue" to proceed to your project's overview page.
  8. Your Project is Ready
    • You can now access your project's settings, add Firebase services, and start integrating Firebase into your applications.
