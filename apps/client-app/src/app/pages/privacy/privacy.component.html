Cookies: how do we use them?

  1. What is a cookie?
    A cookie is a text file that may be stored on a terminal when viewing an online service with a browser. A cookie file allows its issuer to recognize the terminal concerned each time it accesses digital content containing cookies from the same issuer during its validity period.
    In any case, cookies stored on your browser terminal with your consent are deleted 13 months after being stored on your terminal.
